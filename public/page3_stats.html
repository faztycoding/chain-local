Analysis & Recommendations

How to read this chart:

  • Points between UCL and LCL: Process is in control - normal variation
  • Points above UCL: Abnormally high defect rate - investigate root cause (materials, machine, operator)
  • Points below LCL: Abnormally low defect rate - find out why and replicate
  • Trends: 7 consecutive points rising/falling indicate a process shift

Recommendations after collecting data:

  • Use Power BI to create detailed dashboards from the SQLite database
  • Compare defect rates between different chain colors and sizes
  • Track weekly/monthly trends to improve production processes
  • Set up alerts when defect rate exceeds acceptable thresholds
