Data Analysis & Recommendations

How to interpret this chart:

  • Points within UCL and LCL: Process is in statistical control - normal variation.
  • Points above UCL: Unusually high defect rate - investigate cause (material, machine, operator).
  • Points below LCL: Unusually low defect rate - identify what went right to replicate.
  • Trends: 7+ consecutive points trending up/down indicate a process shift.

Recommended Actions after receiving data:

  • Use Power BI to create drill-down dashboards from the SQLite database.
  • Compare defect rates across different chain colors and sizes.
  • Track weekly/monthly trends to optimize production parameters.
  • Set up alerts when defect rate exceeds acceptable limits.
